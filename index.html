About us

- WOMAN AT THE WELL -

is dedicated to building up women through the teaching of biblical principles


Our hope is to see women filled with joy and God's love to be a positive influence for their families and the larger community

OUR STORY

IN THE BEGINNING

Woman at the Well began in 2016 when Hai Vu and Chi Hoang were compelled by the same hope: to see women break through personal barriers and become faithful and confident women of God, who would powerfully impact their communities.

An inspiration came through Revelations 3:20 where God “stands at the door and knocks, and if anyone hears His voice and opens the door, He will come in and eat with that person and they with Him” (NIV). By God’s leading, specific women’s names were selected and prayed over before handcrafted invitations were sent out as knocks on their hearts . The first small table of 8 were served a formal luncheon, which was delightfully decorated and encouragingly exquisite.

Over time, different women came together and were prompted to dress up beautifully as God’s beloved and fellowship as friends sharing a meal. Beginning with affirming grandmothers, then bridging intergenerational women, and now connecting women across culture and race, what started out as a small gathering has now become an organization that intentionally works to build up all women in the love of God.

Like Hai and Chi, Trang Le, one of the first attendee, also experienced the deep love of Jesus who sees and knows every blemish, thirst, and painful past, yet fully accepts us and offers us living water that never runs dry and always satisfies. And like the Samaritan woman at the well, the three were not only given new life in Christ, but also transformed in such a way that compels them to serve women with the same love (John 4:1-42).

PHOTOS

Meet the team.

HAI THI VU

Ministry Director

Hai loves living in big cities! She grew up in Saigon, Vietnam, before immigrating to the US in 1984. She rooted herself in Bellevue, WA for over 32 years. Hai is a joyful grandmother to 5 beautiful grandchildren. She was inspired by God’s love to serve women with a beautiful experience of sharing a meal, learning God’s Word, and supporting each other through prayer.
God ignited a big dream for starting Woman at the Well which came into fruition in 2016. He strengthened her faith and provided her the ability to connect across generations, cultures, & family types. When Hai is not serving women, she enjoys spending time with her family, reading God’s word, and making beautiful cross-stitching designs.

TRANG LE

Secretary

Trang can be distinguished by her contagious laughter and creative storytelling. As a child, she loved reading all types of books and dreamed of being a writer one day. When she was saved, Jesus filled her life with the all-time greatest story: The Gospel of His Redeeming Love! She enjoys working alongside others to build up the next generation, support families, and promote health for communities.
In her spare time, she likes to explore the outdoors on feet as an avid runner, cook experimentally, and read biographies. God graciously opened doors for her to serve, learn and grow with the Woman at the Well ministry since 2016.

CHI HOANG

Treasurer

Chi is a community worker with passions for education, design, and nature. She enjoys working on creative collaboration projects and eating noodle soups. Since coming to believe in Jesus as a youth, she has loved the adventure of working with various churches, communities, and organizations in exploring and seeing the unfolding of the “mystery of His will according to His good pleasure, which God proposed in Christ” (Ephesians 1:9). She has worked with Woman at the Well since it started in 2016.
