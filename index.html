Meet the team.

HAI THI VU

Ministry Director

Hai Vu loves living in big cities! She grew up in Saigon, the capital of southern Vietnam, before immigrating to the United States in 1984. She rooted herself in the greater Seattle area for the past 32 years and enjoyed a fruitful time in raising her family and is now being a joyful grandmother to 5 beautiful grandchildren.

She found a passion for God’s Word and was inspired by His love to serve women with a beautiful experience of sharing a meal, learning God’s Word, and supporting each other through prayers. God gave her a big dream for starting Woman at the Well and opened a door for the ministry to be established in 2016.

He strengthened her faith and provided her the ability to connect across generations, cultures, & family types. When Hai is not serving women, she enjoys spending time with her family, reading God’s word, and making beautiful cross-stitching designs.
